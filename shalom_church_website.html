Welcome

We are a Christ-centered, Bible-believing, Spirit-filled, and multicultural Eritrean church located in Canada. Join us every Sunday for worship, prayer, teaching of the Word, and Christian fellowship.

“For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them.” – Matthew 18:20

About Us

Our History

Shalom Gospel Missionary Church was started in 2022 to serve Eritrean believers and newcomers in Canada. We began as “Shalom Church” and have grown into a united family by God's grace.

Our Mission

To share the gospel of Jesus Christ, help people grow in faith, and support newcomers with love, teaching, and discipleship.

Our Vision

  • Worship God in truth and spirit
  • Support each other with love
  • Reach the lost and the needy with Christ’s light

Newcomers

Welcome! If you're new to Canada or the church, we welcome you with love. At Shalom, you will find:

  • A caring family in Christ
  • Multilingual worship (Tigrigna & English)
  • Coffee and fellowship after service
  • Support with transportation and settlement
